60 * Atmel AVR

62 *
63 * Method #1: Disable/Enable interrupts using simple instructions. After critical section, interrupts
64 * will be enabled even if they were disabled before entering the critical section.
65 *
66 * Method #2: Disable/Enable interrupts by preserving the state of interrupts. In other words, if
67 * interrupts were disabled before entering the critical section, they will be disabled when
68 * leaving the critical section. Here's what happens in the macro below :
69 *
70 * OS_ENTER_CRITICAL:
71 *
72 * input SREG to a free register, Rn
73 * push Rn
74 *
75 * OS_EXIT_CRITICAL:
76 *
77 * pop to a free register, Rn
78 * output Rn to SREG
79 *
80 *
81 **********************************************************************************************************
82 */
